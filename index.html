About this event

The Nairobi Summer Farmers Market is your go-to destination for fresh, locally sourced produce, artisan foods, and handcrafted goods. Held at the iconic Ngong Racecourse, this multi-day market brings together over 80 vendors from across Kenya.

Expect fresh fruits, vegetables, artisan cheeses, live cooking demos by top Nairobi chefs, children's activities, and free parking on site.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a ticket? ▼
Find the event you want, click on it, then click "Get Tickets." You'll pay via M-Pesa STK Push — a prompt will be sent to your phone. Confirm payment and your ticket will be emailed instantly.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Refund policies are set by event organizers. Check the event page for the refund policy. If the event was cancelled, you will receive an automatic M-Pesa refund within 5–7 business days.
How do I list a free event? ▼
Listing a free event on EventsKenya is 100% free. Click "Create Events" in the navigation, fill out the form, and publish. Your event goes live immediately.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept M-Pesa (Safaricom), Airtel Money, and major debit/credit cards. M-Pesa is the most popular and fastest payment method on our platform.
How do organizers receive payouts? ▼
Organizers receive payouts via M-Pesa or bank transfer within 3 business days after the event ends. Track your earnings in your organizer dashboard.
