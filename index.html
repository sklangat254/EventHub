About This Event

The Atlanta Summer Farmers Market is your premier destination for fresh, locally sourced produce, artisan foods, handcrafted goods, and community spirit. Held at Patterson Park in southwest Atlanta, this three-day market brings together vendors from across Georgia.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply as a vendor? ▼
Click “Apply as Vendor,” fill in your details, and submit. No payment is required at this stage — you will receive payment instructions by email once your application is approved.
What are the booth prices? ▼
Service/Product: $65/day (10×10), $70/day (10×20), $90/day (Food Truck). Food Vendors: $73/day (10×10), $78/day (10×20), $90/day (Food Truck). All prices are per day.
When do I pay for my booth? ▼
Payment is only required after your application is approved. You will receive Chime payment instructions by email. Send to $Laura-Ramirez-195 and payment is due within 72 hours of acceptance.
What is the cancellation/refund policy? ▼
Vendor fees are non-refundable once payment is confirmed. If the event is canceled due to rain, a decision is made by 7 AM and you’ll be notified by text.
What time can vendors set up? ▼
2 hours before the event opens. Tables and chairs are provided. You’re responsible for signage and keeping your booth clean throughout the event.
